How I Work

From transformation to consultancy.

Every AI or digital transformation eventually arrives at the same question: are the people doing the work ready for it, equipped for it, and is the work itself still structured right? Fifteen years on the technology side of that question means I've watched it get asked — and answered badly — more times than I can count. Learning consultancy is what happens when someone answers it properly.

A recent engagement — for a regional trade organisation redesigning its RFQ (request-for-quotation) process — moved through all three stages in sequence:

01 — Readiness

Where do we actually stand?

A Workforce & AI Readiness Diagnostic and Maturity Scorecard, establishing where the organisation's RFQ process actually stood before recommending anything.

02 — Capability Building

Can people use it?

A validated workplace learning implementation roadmap, with 100% of affected staff trained to interpret and deploy it.

03 — Redesign

Is the job itself right?

Job roles re-scoped from manual data extraction toward high-value validation and strategic decision-making — validated role documents, with training on the new roles.

How engagements run

I lead as project manager and integrator; an HR specialist and an AI engineer cover the specialist work. The rule holds at every layer — AI drafts the diagnostic, the roadmap, and the role descriptions; the people in the room decide what's right for their organisation.

Module 01 · Account Development & Pipeline Intelligence

AI-Augmented Prospect Research & Account Prioritisation

"Account list uploaded. Signals scored. Priority set."

AI drafts

Produces a prioritised account ranking with scoring rationale from the uploaded target account list, and flags accounts where incomplete firmographic data is affecting the score.

You decide

Validate where the ranking diverges from commercial intuition, and identify which signal is being over-weighted before committing outreach resources.

Module 09 · Proposal Excellence & Client Engagement

AI-Augmented Account Review & Renewal Briefing

"Account history reviewed. Risk surfaced. Renewal positioned."

AI drafts

A one-page account review brief — renewal risk signals, expansion opportunities, and three recommended next actions — from the uploaded account history or interaction log.

You decide
